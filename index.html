Our Projects.

Zmejka-Huejka

This is a variation on the classic snake. You should travel through the virtual world as a snake in search of penises, eat them and grow and evolve due to this.

Penis Clicker

In this exciting game you have to extract sperm from different penises for the needs of pharmacology, sperm banks, science and to fight depression and loneliness. And thus make this world a better place.

Three Eggs in Row

Testicles are good. But it's even better if there are three. Collect all the testicles into triads of testicles so that the testicles are good.

The Witcher 4

Continuation of the famous game in the universe of Andrzej Sapkowski. Tells about the history of Buttercup before meeting the witcher. How he wanted to become an engineer, but something went wrong.
